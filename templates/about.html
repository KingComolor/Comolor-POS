Empowering Kenyan Retailers

We're on a mission to modernize retail operations across Kenya with affordable, powerful point-of-sale technology.

Our Story

Comolor POS was born from a simple observation: small and medium retailers in Kenya were struggling with outdated point-of-sale systems that didn't understand their unique needs.

Traditional POS systems were either too expensive, too complex, or didn't integrate with the payment methods Kenyan customers preferred - especially MPesa.

We set out to build a solution that would be affordable, easy to use, and designed specifically for the Kenyan market. Today, Comolor POS serves hundreds of retailers across the country.

Our platform helps business owners focus on what they do best - serving their customers - while we handle the technology behind the scenes.

Our Mission

To provide affordable, intuitive point-of-sale technology that helps Kenyan retailers grow their businesses and serve their communities better.

Our Vision

A Kenya where every retailer, regardless of size, has access to world-class technology that helps them compete and thrive in the digital economy.
